Excercise Regimen

Cardio

Frequency: 3-4 times per week
Duration: 30 minutes per session

Activities:

Brisk Walking: Walking at a moderate pace to elevate heart rate.
Cycling: Riding a bike outdoors or using a stationary bike.
Swimming: Swimming laps or water aerobics for cardiovascular fitness. Strength Training

Strength Training

Frequency: 3-4 times per week
Activities: Bodyweight exercises focusing on functional movements.

Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, lower hips towards the floor, keeping knees behind toes, push back to starting position.

Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Instructions: Start in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, lower body towards the floor, keeping elbows close to body, push back to starting position.

Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Instructions: Hold dumbbells in each hand, hinge at the hips, keeping back flat, pull elbows up towards ceiling, squeezing shoulder blades together, lower back down with control. to heels, engage core muscles.

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water throughout the day, aiming for at least 8 glasses. Include herbal teas or infused water for variety.

Meal Preparation:

Plan meals ahead of time to ensure balanced nutrition. Keep healthy snacks readily available for convenience.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Consistency:

Maintain regular eating and exercise habits to support overall health.Stick to a consistent meal and workout schedule.

Track Progress:

Maintain awareness of portion sizes and overall calorie intake. Listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues.

Mindset:

Embrace moderation in diet and exercise for long-term sustainability. Focus on overall health and well-being rather than strict calorie counting.
