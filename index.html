Understanding BMI Categories

BMI categorizes individuals into five primary classifications: underweight, normal weight, overweight, obese, and extreme obesity. Underweight individuals typically possess a BMI below 18.5, whereas those falling within the range of 18.5 to 24.9 are classified as normal weight. Individuals with a BMI between 25 and 29.9 are considered overweight, while those with a BMI of 30 or higher fall into the obese category. Extreme obesity is characterized by a BMI of 35 or higher, indicating a significantly heightened risk of severe health complications.
